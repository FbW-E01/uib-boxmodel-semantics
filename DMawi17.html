Semantic. MarkUP

Semantic HTML elements are those that clearly describe their meaning in a human and machine-readable way.

What are Semantic Elements?

Elements such as header, footer and article are all considered semantic because they accurately describe the purpose of the element and the type of content that is inside them.

Why use semantic elements?

  • It is much easier to read.
  • It has greater accessibility.
  • SEO, influence page search ranking
  • Overall, it lead to more consistent code.

List of new semantic elements

The semantic elements added in HTML5 are:

  • article
  • aside
  • details
  • figcaption
  • figure
  • footer
  • header
  • main
  • mark
  • nav
  • section
  • summary
  • time
